About us

Poland Veterinary Sp. Z o.o. is a company from the veterinary industry. We produce milk replacers, pet food and animals skin care preparations. In the future we would like to expand the scope of our activities and include veterinary medicine.

What makes us different is that we connect European markets with the countries of the Persian Gulf, creating an international company and an international mission.

Our employees are highly qualified veterinary and zootechnical specialists who perfectly understand the needs of domestic and breeding animals.

If you are interested in cooperation, please contact us.

News

On march 2019 we started our partnership with Lacto Production – a French company which specializes in high quality milk replacers for calves.
